Introduction

Shadow Fight 2 is a role-playing fighting game produced by Nekki in 2013 thats available on both Android and iOS. Shadow fight 2 gained popularity from its unique one-on-one combat style that also attracted players who enjoy playing Mortal Kombat. Shadow fight 2 provides the user a unique experience with an amazing story, levels, and characters without needing a powerful smartphone to play the game smoothly. Shadow fight 2 graphics are simple and playable with characters presenting as shadowy 2D figures with an aesthetic scenery as the background. The game also includes powerful soundtracks that let the players feel connected with the game. Shadow fight 2 provides 7 chapters that the player needs to play in before completing the game, with each chapter having a final boss before allowing the player to move on to the next chapter. Each chapter has a unique story that tells a lot about the character you are playing as and its mission in the story. As you progress through the chapters, you get to learn more about the character you are playing as, while you have to fight against harder opponents and earn better equipments

Characters

  • Shadow - The Main Character of the story who seeks to defeat all of the 7 demons
  • May - Shadow's Girlfriend and an expert blacksmith
  • Sensei - Shadow's teacher
  • Lynx - Leader of an assassin group called "The Order". The first demon Shadow encountered
  • Hermit - An old sensei capable of generating dark magic. The second demon Shadow encountered
  • Butcher - A bandit who raises lost children and trains them to become violent and aggressive like him. The third demon Shadow encountered
  • Wasp - Daughter of the Pirate King. The fourth demon Shadow encountered
  • Widow - A woman who has the ability to attract men with her beauty, making them her slaves. The fifth demon Shadow encountered
  • Shogun - The ruler of Ivory City who seeks to kill Shadow, claiming him to be the "Prince". The sixth demon Shadow encountered
  • Titan - The main antagonist of the story who is known to enslave many worlds in the multiverse. The last demon Shadow encountered

Story Summary

Shadow Fight 2 took place in Japan's Tokugawa period in the late 1600s. The story starts with a great invincible warrior named Shadow travelling the land in search of a worthy opponent, til he stumbled upon the gates of shadows. It was told that it is forbidden to open the gates of shadows since it is a portal that opens the multiverse, leading to another world, but Shadow opened the gates, not knowing what curse he had just released into the world. As the gates opened, the demons from the other world have been released. They rushed upon Shadow, tearing his flesh, draining his soul, turning him into a shadow. After Shadow realized the threat he has brought into the world, he has a plan to defeat all of the 7 demons with the help of his sensei and his girlfriend May to save the world and obtain his physical form back. After defeating 6 demons, Shadow has to face the strongest demon named Titan, an emperor who is known to enslave worlds in the multiverse, who also kidnapped May and turned her evil. After saving May and an exhausting battle against Titan, Shadow destroyed Titan following the collapse of the Gates of Shadows, destroying the portal to the other world.
