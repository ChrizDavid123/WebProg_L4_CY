The Chicken Shawarma

The Chicken Shawarma (introduced during the 18th or 19th century ottoman empire) is a Middle Eastern Dish consisting of many different ingredients all rolled up inside a Pita Bread. It is a mixture of different ingredients including boneless chicken cut into many small pieces and a bunch of spices all wrapped up inside a bread which gives the dish a unique and amazing taste.

Shawarma consists of many different ingredients such as

  • Boneless Chicken
  • Garlic Sauce
  • Fries
  • Pita Bread
  • shawarma spice
  • lemon
  • soy sauce
  • Salt

How to create a chicken shawarma...

  1. Add soy sauce, salt, shawarma spice and lemon juice in a bowl and mix them all together
  2. Slice the boneless chicken into thin pieces and add them into the bowl of already mixed ingredients for marination (cover the bowl and leave in the fridge for 30 minutes)
  3. Fry the marinated chicken til its perfectly cooked
  4. Once its cooked, cut the cooked chicken into tiny pieces
  5. Prepare the pita bread and insert a fair amount of garlic sauce in it. Make sure to mix it around the inside of the pita bread
  6. Instert Fries, Pickles, and chicken inside the pita bread
  7. Roll it up
  8. ENJOY!
